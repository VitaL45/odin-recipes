Omelette Recipe

Description:

An omelet is a dish you might order for breakfast or brunch â€” it's kind of like a folded pancake made of beaten eggs, sometimes with the addition of vegetables, cheese, or meat. Yum. A good omelet is a little bit fluffy, but firm enough to contain its filling.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp onion finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp capsicum finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato optional
  • 1/8 tsp Salt or as needed
  • 1 green chilli chopped
  • 1 pinch turmeric
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves finely chopped

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop Onions, tomatoes, chilies, coriander leaves and capsicum finely.
  2. Mix together with salt and turmeric.
  3. Break the eggs to bowl and check for shells
  4. Add oil to the pan and heat it on a medium flame. Do not let the pan turn very hot, it will brown the omelette without cooking well.
  5. Pour the egg mixture, allow it to cook.
  6. When the base firms up, flip it to the other side and cook till done> Sprinkle crushed pepper or chilli powder if you like.
  7. Serve the Omelette hot with rice, roti or bread.
